Accessibility Statement

Boboh's Portfolio strives to make this website accessible in accordance with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA. This accessibility statement applies to the website accessible under the domain https://mcyum.github.io/portfolio/.

Status of Compliance with Requirements

This website is largely compliant with the requirements of WCAG 2.1 AA, according to self-assessment. Efforts have been made to ensure high accessibility, including semantic HTML structure, keyboard operability, alternative texts for images, sufficient color contrasts (especially in the high-contrast theme), and consideration of `prefers-reduced-motion`.

This accessibility statement was created on May 29, 2025.

The assessment was carried out by self-assessment with the help of tools such as the WAVE Evaluation Tool and Accessibility Insights for Web (accessScan).

Not Fully Accessible Content

Despite our efforts, the following areas may have limitations:

  • CSS Art (page `css-art.html`): The CSS artwork ("Creepy Monkey Face") is primarily visual. Although an `aria-label` is provided for the container describing the image, the complex visual representation cannot be fully conveyed in text form without losing the artistic aspect of the CSS demonstration. According to the assignment, this page is explicitly not required to be responsive, and its accessibility regarding the art itself is inherently limited.
  • Color Contrasts in Specific Theme Combinations: Although a high-contrast theme is offered, some elements in the standard themes (Light/Dark) might exist whose contrast ratio narrowly falls short of AA requirements. This is continuously monitored.
  • External Content: For content embedded from third parties (e.g., Google Fonts, Font Awesome CDN), complete assurance of their accessibility cannot be guaranteed, although these providers generally adhere to high standards themselves.
