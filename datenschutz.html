Privacy Policy

1. Data Protection at a Glance

General Information

The following information provides a simple overview of what happens to your personal data when you visit this website. Personal data is any data with which you can be personally identified. For this student portfolio project, as little data as possible is collected, and no external tracking services are actively integrated.

Data Collection on This Website

Who is responsible for data collection on this website?

Data processing on this website is carried out by the website operator named in the Legal Notice.

How do we collect your data?

Within this portfolio, data is primarily collected as follows:

  • Voluntary Information: If you fill out and submit the favorites form (however, the data is not stored or processed server-side here, as it is purely a front-end demo).
  • Automatically Collected Technical Data: When visiting the website, technical data such as browser type, operating system, or time of page access may be collected by the web server (e.g., GitLab Pages). The operator of this portfolio has limited or no direct access to this server-side logging.
  • LocalStorage: The theme switcher script uses your browser's LocalStorage to save your preferred theme setting (Dark, Light, High Contrast) for future visits. This does not store personal identifiers and serves purely for user convenience.

What do we use your data for?

The automatically collected technical data is generally used to ensure the error-free provision of the website and to improve the service. The theme setting stored in LocalStorage serves to personalize your user experience on this site.

What rights do you have regarding your data?

You have the right at any time to receive information free of charge about the origin, recipient, and purpose of your stored personal data. You also have a right to request the correction or deletion of this data, provided there are no legal retention obligations to the contrary. For LocalStorage, you can delete the data yourself at any time in your browser settings.

2. Hosting

This website is hosted via GitLab Pages. The provider is GitLab Inc., 268 Bush Street, #350, San Francisco, CA 94104, USA. When you visit our website, GitLab collects various log files including your IP addresses. For details, please refer to GitLab's privacy policy: https://about.gitlab.com/privacy/.

The use of GitLab Pages is in the interest of a professional and secure provision of this student portfolio.

3. Cookies and LocalStorage

This website does not use cookies for tracking or analysis. As mentioned above, the browser's `localStorage` is used to store the theme setting. This is technically comparable to a functional cookie but does not store data that would allow tracking across different websites.

You can set your browser to inform you about the setting of cookies (or the use of LocalStorage) and allow this only in individual cases, exclude the acceptance for certain cases or generally, and activate automatic deletion when closing the browser.
